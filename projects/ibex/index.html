Inspired by various mountain wanderers

Elk

Named after Elk, a species of deer family identified by their majestic antlers, these boards are all about aggression and dominance.

A blend of big mountain attributes and all-terrain aggression, the Elk finds the sweet spot for everything from steep lines and spontaneous slashes to wind-lips, pillows, and side hits.

Ibex

Named after Ibex, a species of wild mountain goats distinguished by their large recurved horns and agonistic behaviour, these boards are all about power and control with agility.

A combination of Balanced Freeride Geometry and the forgiving flex of PurePop Camber delivers float and playfulness, while the twin tip shape and centered stance allow for freestyle exploration in all directions.
